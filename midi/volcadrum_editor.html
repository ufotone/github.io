Firmware Update (audio)

The volca drum firmware is audio (WAV) data. Put the unit into update mode, connect your PC's headphone output to the unit's SYNC IN, and play the WAV below.

  1. Turn the unit off
  2. Hold FUNC and power on to enter update standby
  3. Set volume near maximum and play the WAV below
  4. Do not touch the unit until transfer completes

Channel mode setup (on the unit)

Hold [REC] and power on to enter setup mode. The lit state of step buttons 1-6 indicates the mode.

■ Split Channel (multi Ch)
Step buttons 1-6 all lit = Part1-6 controlled individually on separate MIDI channels 1-6.
If only one is lit, press any of [1]-[6] to switch to all lit.
→ Part1=Ch1, Part2=Ch2 … Part6=Ch6 (editor: Ch Mode = Split)

■ Single Channel (omni)
The mode where only one of step buttons 1-6 is lit.
Use the keyboard keys to select 1-16 MIDI channel (all parts sound on the same MIDI channel).

■ Save
Press [REC] to save; the unit restarts and applies the setting.
